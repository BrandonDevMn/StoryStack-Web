About StoryShelf

StoryShelf helps you decide what to read next from your ever-growing to-be-read (TBR) list. Simply paste your book list, and let StoryShelf randomly select three prioritized reading recommendations for you.

Perfect for book lovers who struggle with choice paralysis when facing their towering book collections!

How It Works

1. Add your TBR list (one book per line)

2. Tap "Pick My Next Books"

3. Get three curated picks: your priority read, backup choice, and alternative option

4. Don't like the selection? Just pick again!
